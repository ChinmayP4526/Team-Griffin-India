Chasing the Blue


Where Dreams takes Flight

What We Do

Our Extremely skilled aeronautics team can build any aircraft from scratch and compete amongst Top National Teams in the SAE Aero Design Challenges.

How We Do

Our Experienced Team has been internally assigned to various domains that work cohesively to make our models come reality. All analysis is done in house to prove and validate design to be then handed over the manufacturing and avionics experts to produce the aircraft.

Why We Do

We believe that every young enthusiast has the capability of making something new and innovative.Team Griffin is the home where we get to make and explore the world never seen before and what it take for your model to reach the skies.
